BoarBot

A gacha-like game based around boars! Collect, buy, sell, and compete with others for the top spots on several different leaderboards!

COLLECT

BoarBot has dozens of unique boars for you to collect! Some are easy to get, some are extremely rare drops, and some can be obtained during events!

POWER UP

Bring some power to your collecting capabilities by participating in Powerup Events! Powerups have a wide range of abilities for you to use and discover!

COMPETE

Have a competitive spirit? You can show it through the several leaderboards that BoarBot shows! Players that have a top spot on a leaderboard also get badge!

BUY AND SELL

Interact with a full-fledged economy by setting up buy orders and sell offers! This is a great place to see and purchase boars you don't have!
